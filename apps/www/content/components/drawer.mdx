## Exports

`DrawerPortal`, `DrawerOverlay`, `DrawerClose`, `DrawerHandle`, `DrawerFooter` are also exported. `DrawerContent` includes a drag handle and glass panel styling.

Vaul `Root` props apply: `open`, `onOpenChange`, `direction`, `dismissible`, `modal`, `snapPoints`, etc. See [Vaul docs](https://github.com/emilkowalski/vaul).
