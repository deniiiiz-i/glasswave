## Additional primitives

`MenubarCheckboxItem`, `MenubarRadioGroup`, `MenubarRadioItem`, `MenubarLabel`, `MenubarShortcut`, `MenubarSub`, `MenubarSubTrigger`, `MenubarSubContent`, `MenubarGroup` match Radix Menubar patterns.

Content panels use the same full-bleed item highlights as `DropdownMenu`.
