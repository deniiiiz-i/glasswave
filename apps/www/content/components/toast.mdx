
## Position

Use the `position` prop to control where the toast appears on the screen. Default duration is 5 seconds.

```tsx
<Toast message="Top left" position="top-left" />
<Toast message="Top center" position="top-center" />
<Toast message="Top right" position="top-right" />
<Toast message="Bottom left" position="bottom-left" />
<Toast message="Bottom center" position="bottom-center" />
<Toast message="Bottom right" position="bottom-right" />
```

Available values: `"top-left"` | `"top-center"` | `"top-right"` | `"bottom-left"` | `"bottom-center"` | `"bottom-right"`
